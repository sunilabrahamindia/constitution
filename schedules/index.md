## Schedules of the Constitution

The Schedules are an important part of the Constitution of India. Each Schedule contains lists, tables or detailed provisions that support the Articles and Parts. They cover subjects such as powers of government, allocation of seats, forms of oaths, administration of tribal areas and distribution of legislative authority. This index lists the Schedules in numerical order.
